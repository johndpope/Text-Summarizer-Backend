Allied-Signal said Bearing Acquisition is a newly-formed  corporation owned by an investors group organized by Harold S.
Wells Fargo Bank provided senior debt financing to Bearing  Acquisition and Donaldson Lufkin and Jenrette provided bridge  financing in the form of subordinated notes, preferred stock  and common stock in an amount sufficient to fund the purchase  price, Allied-Signal added.
