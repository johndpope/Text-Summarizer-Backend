Traded volumes for U.K. Grain and potato  futures in February were down on the previous month while  pigmeat and pig cash settlement futures were higher, official  figures show.
Soymeal futures trade totalled 76,340 tonnes against 90,680  in January, and value declined to nine mln stg from 10 mln.
Pig cash settlement futures saw 201 contracts traded,  against 19 in January, and the value rose to 659,864 stg from  119,610 stg.
