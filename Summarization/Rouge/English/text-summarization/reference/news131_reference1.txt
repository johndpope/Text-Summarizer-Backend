A total 118,350 tonnes of current series  white sugar received export rebates of a maximum 46.496  European Currency Units (Ecus) per 100 kilos at today's  European Community (EC) tender, the U.K. Intervention Board  said.
Earlier today, London traders had declined to give a  projected view on the level of subsidy although some said total  tonnage awards would be around 60,000 tonnes.
Last week saw 102,350 tonnes whites authorised for export  under licences to end-Sep at the higest ever rebate of 46.864  European Currency Units (Ecus) per 100 kilos.
