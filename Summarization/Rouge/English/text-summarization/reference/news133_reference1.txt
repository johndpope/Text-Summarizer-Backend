Moisture  for early rice planting is adequate to abundant in most areas,  and inundative rain in Guangdong and southern Jiangxi may have  washed out some rice fields requiring replanting.
Light showers in southern Sichuan increased topsoil  moisture for corn planting, which likely is off to a slow start  due to earlier dryness, it said.
In the North China Plain, dry weather covered winter wheat  in the vegetative stage, and rain is needed to meet increasing  crop moisture requirements, it said.
