Japan's unadjusted consumer price index  (base 1985) fell 0.4 pct to 99.7 in January from the previous  month, the government's Management and Coodination Agency said.
Food costs rose in January from December but prices fell  for clothing, footwear and utilities, causing the overall  decline for the month.
The unadjusted consumer price index for the Tokyo area  (base 1985) was down 0.1 pct in mid-February from a month  earlier at 100.2, reflecting lower prices for food, clothing  and footwear.
