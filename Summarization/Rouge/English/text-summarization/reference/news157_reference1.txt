the venezuelan state aluminum company  venalum is negotiating a total of 123.9 mln dlr in credits from  abroad for a planned expansion of production faciities, finance  minister manuel azpurua said.
Azpurua spoke to reporters after meeting representatives of  the kreditanstalt fur wiederaufbau (kfw) bank of germany, who  tommorrow will sign a agreement to grant 100 mln marks (54.9  mln US dlrs) in credits to venalum.
The credits would go to the installation of a fifth  production line in venalum, one of three state aluminum  companies, which produces primarily for the export market.
