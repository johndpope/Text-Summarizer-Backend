Quantech Electronics Corp said  it is investigating available means of raising additional funds  needed to finance continuing operations, but there is no  assurance that it will succeed.
Quantech said it has received waivers from Marine Midland  Banks Inc &lt;MM> through May 26 on covenants in its loan  agreement.
The company said Marine Midland has agreed to advance it an  additional working capital loan that will be personally  guaranteed by chairman Henry Ginsburg.
Loans from Marine  Midland are secured by substantially all company assets.
