French official reserves rose 258 mln  francs in May to 420.50 billion francs from 420.24 billion at  the end of April, the Finance Ministry said.
Foreign currency holdings fell 7.28 billion francs to  124.58 billion while ECU holdings fell by 5.55 billion francs  to 58.52 billion due to the repayment of part of France's debt  owed to the FECOM and Stabilisation Fund operations, the  ministry said.
Claims on the IMF rose 1.14 billion francs to 18.97  billion, including a 385 mln franc increase in France's reserve  position with the IMF and a 751 mln franc rise in holdings of  Special Drawing Rights.
