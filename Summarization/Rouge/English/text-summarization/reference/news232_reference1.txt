U.S. oil demand as measured by  products supplied fell 2.6 pct in the four weeks ended April  three to 15.73 mln barrels per day (bpd) from 16.16 mln in the  same period a year ago, the Energy Information Administration  (EIA) said.
Gasoline demand averaged 6.76 mln bpd, off 3.1 pct from  6.98 mln last year, while residual fuel demand was 1.15 mln  bpd, off 16.9 pct from 1.39 mln, the EIA said.
