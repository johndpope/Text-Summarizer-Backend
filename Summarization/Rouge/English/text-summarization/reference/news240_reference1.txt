Singapore's United Industrial Corp Ltd  (UIC) has agreed in principle to inject 16 mln dlrs in  convertible loan stock into &lt;Teck Hock and Co (Pte) Ltd>, a  creditor bank official said.
Teck Hock owes more than 100 mln Singapore dlrs and since  last December the banks have been allowing the company to  postpone loan repayments while they try to find an investor.
