For the year it reported a net loss of 6.5 mln dlrs or 70  cts per share as a result of writedowns in the book value of  its oil and gas properties in the first two quarters.
For the nine months ended Dec 31, 1985, it reported a loss  of 63.4 mln dlrs or 6.54 dlrs per share on revenues of 47.6 mln  dlrs, which it said was a result of noncash writedowns of oil  and gas properties.
For the year ended March 31, 1985, Cenergy reported net  income of 3,705,000 dlrs or 36 cts per share on revenues of 71  mln dlrs.
The company said its reserves during the year fell to five  mln barrels from 6.4 mln barrels of oil and to 60.1 bilion  cubic feet of gas from 63.7 BCF.
