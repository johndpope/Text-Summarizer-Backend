The company said eight of nine holes drilled on the new ore  zone have an average grade of 0.046 ounces of gold per ton and  2.1 ounces of silver per ton.
The study said ore reserves including dillution were  3,302,000 tons at 0.77 ounces of gold per ton at a cut off  grade of 0.026 ounces per ton and stripping ratio of 4.24 to  one.
