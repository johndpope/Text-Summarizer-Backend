The Treasury Department is due to  release funds for the Brazilian Coffee Institute, IBC, to pay  for the coffee purchased from local producers, the IBC said in  a statement.
IBC production director Oripes Gomes said in the statement  that payment would be made within the official guarantee  prices.
Gomes said in the statement there have been no problems in  the concession of funds by the Treasury for payment of the  coffee delivered to the IBC warehouses by the producers.
