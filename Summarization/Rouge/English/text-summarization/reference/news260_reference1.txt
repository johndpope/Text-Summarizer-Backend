Ohio Mattress Co said itr has  executed a definitive agreement to acquire Sealy Mattress Co of  Michigan Inc, the Detroit licensee of Ohio Mattress's 82 pct  owned Sealy Inc subsidiary in a transaction that also involves  a settlement between Sealy and Michigan Sealy.
The company said on completion of the acquisition, the  Sealy stock owned by Michigan Sealy will be redeemed, raising  Ohio Mattress' interest in Sealy to 93 pct.
Under the acquisition agreement, the company said Sealy  will enter into a cash settlement of its litigation with  Michigan Sealy.
