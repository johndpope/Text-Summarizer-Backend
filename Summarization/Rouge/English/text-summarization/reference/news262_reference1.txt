First Bank Systems Inc, in reporting  higher first quarter net, said that if interest is not paid on  the 140 mln dlrs in Brazilian loans and nine mln dlrs in  Ecuadorian loans for the rest of 1987, its profits for the  entire year will be cut by 6.9 mln dlrs.
The banking firm said the non-performing status of these  loans cut first quarter net by 1.7 mln dlrs.
Earlier it reported first quarter profits of 57.4 mln dlrs  or 95 cts a share, up from 46.6 mln dlrs or 80 cts a share in  the year-ago period.
