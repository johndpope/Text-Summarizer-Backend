Japan will pay five pct less for the  Indian iron ore it imports in fiscal 1987/88 starting April 1  than the average 18 dlrs a tonne it paid in 1986/87, a  government trade official told Reuters.
He said India had agreed to export to Japan about 23 mln  tonnes of iron ore in 1987/88, about the same as in the current  year.
Government officials said India's total ore exports are  likely to rise to between 33 and 34 mln tonnes in 1987/88,  against a provisionally estimated 31 mln in 1986/87.
