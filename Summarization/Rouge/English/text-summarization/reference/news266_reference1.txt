The West German Economics Minister today  denied giving a newspaper interview which quoted him as saying  the state could not continue to pour money into the country's  ailing steel and coal industries.
Economics Ministry spokesman Dieter Vogel said in a  statement Bangemann had contacted him from New Zealand, where  he is attending a General Agreement on Trade and Tariffs (GATT)  ministerial meeting, to deny giving the interview to the  conservative daily Die Welt.
