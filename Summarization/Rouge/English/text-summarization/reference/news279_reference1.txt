Unilever Plc and NV &lt;UN.AS> has issued a  prospectus through investment bankers Goldman Sachs and Co  seeking a buyer for &lt;Stauffer Chemical Co> of the U.S., Which  it acquired with the recent takeover of &lt;Chesebrough-Pond's  Inc>, a Unilever spokesman said.
He noted Unilever has been indicating plans to dispose of  Stauffer, plus some smaller assets of Chesebrough, since the  bid was made in December.
The Stauffer sale prospectus has been sent in recent weeks  to a number of companies expressing interest.
The Unilever spokesman declined to say how much the group  expected to receive for Stauffer.
