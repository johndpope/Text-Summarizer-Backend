Multi-Step also said it will pay 900,000 dlrs to cancel  711,192 of its own shares, which will be acquired from Michael  Penhale and his benficiaries.
The sale, expected to close in April, also calls for  retirement of the unit's 400,000 dlr bank debt, Multi-Step  said.
The unit also has agreed to pay a debt of 400,000 dlrs to  Tarxien Company Ltd, which is 40 pct owned by Multi-Step.
Multi-Step previously said it agreed to acquire the  remaining 60 pct of Tarxien it does not already own.
