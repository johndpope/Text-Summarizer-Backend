Dixons Group PLC said that pursuant to  an agreement with Audio/Video Affiliates Inc, part of an  investor group that made a rival bid for Cyclops Corp &lt;CYL>,  all litigation between them has been dismissed.
As part of the agreement, Dixons said Cyacq Corp, the  Audio/Video investor group that includes Citicorp Capital  Investors Ltd, agreed to "promptly" terminate its existing  tender offer for all outstanding Cyclops common at 92.50 dlrs  per share.
