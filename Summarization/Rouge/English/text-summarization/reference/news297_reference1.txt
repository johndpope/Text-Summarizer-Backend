Borrowings from the private sector by non-bank financial  intermediaries rose by 8.8 pct in the February year from  January's 9.5 pct rise, compared with a 13.6 pct increase in  the previous February year.
In February, borrowings from the private sector by non-bank  financial intermediaries rose by 1.0 pct compared with  January's 0.2 pct increase and the previous February rise of  1.7 pct.
The Reserve Bank last week reported a February M3 growth  rate of 11.2 pct from January's 10.7 pct rise and a previous  annual February increase of 14.0 pct.
