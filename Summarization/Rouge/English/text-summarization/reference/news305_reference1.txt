ConAgra Inc agreed to acquire  Monfort of Colorado Inc in a stock transaction, both companies  said.
Based on ConAgra's closing price of 34 dlrs today, the  transaction is worth about 356.5 mln dlrs.
In fiscal 1986, ConAgra had net income of 105.3 mln  dlrs on sales of 5.9 billion dlrs.
For its fiscal year ending August 1986, Monfort reported  25.1 mln dlrs in earnings on sales of 1.6 billion dlrs.
