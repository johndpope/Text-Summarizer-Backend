The Pillsbury Co said it  filed a registration statement with the Securities and Exchange  Commission for the sale of limited partnership interests in a  second master limited partnership of its Burger King unit's  restaurant properties.
The company said Burger King and another Pillsbury unit,  QSV Properties, will be the master limited partnership's  general partner.
Pillsbury said it expects the interests to be sold to  public investors and listed for trading on the New York Stock  Exchange.
Pillsbury first sold limited partnership interests in  Burger King Investors L.P. in February 1986.
