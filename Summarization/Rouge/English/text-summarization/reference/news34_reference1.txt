USAir Group Inc said it has  acquired 2,292,599 Piedmont Aviation Inc shares, about 9.9 pct  on a fully diluted basis, from Norfolk Southern Corp &lt;NSC>.
USAir said it has been advised by Norfolk Southern that the  company supports the proposed merger and intends to tender all  of its remaining 1,477,829 Piedmont common shares in response  to USAir's tender offer which began today.
USAir said Piedmont has about 23.1 mln common shares on a  fully diluted basis, adding its offer is conditioned on the  tender of at least 9,309,394 shares, representing 40.2 pct of  the oustanding shares on a diluted basis.
