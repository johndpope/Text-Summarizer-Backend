New York investor Asher Edelman and  Dominion Textile Inc &lt;DTX.T>, who are currently seeking to  acquire Burlington Industries Inc in a hostile tender offer,  said they lowered their stake in the company.
In a filing with the Secruties and Exchange Commission, the  Edelman/Dominion group, known as Samjens Acqusition Corp, said  it sold options to buy 258,800 Burlington common shares,  reducing its stake in the company to 3,408,813 shares, or 12.33  pct, from 3,667,313 shares, or 13.3 pct.
