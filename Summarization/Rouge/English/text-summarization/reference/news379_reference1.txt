U.K. Average earnings rose a seasonally  adjusted 6.5 pct in the year to end-April, after increasing by  6.7 pct annually in March, the Employment Department said.
Unit wage costs in U.K. Manufacturing industry rose one pct  in the year to April, after an upward revised rise of 0.7 pct  in the period to March, the Department of Employment added.
In the three months to end-April, such costs were 0.9 pct  higher, year-on-year, compared with a rise of 1.2 pct in the  three months to March.
Manufacturing productivity in April was 6.1 pct higher than  a year ago after a downward revised 6.8 pct increase in March.
