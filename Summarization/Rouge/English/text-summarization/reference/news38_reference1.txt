Iomega Corp announced it expected  revenues for the first quarter of 1987 to be significantly  lower than planned and that it anticipated a loss for the  quarter.
In its annual report to be mailed to stockholders tomorrow,  the company will announce its first quarter loss will be in  excess of 10 mln dlrs primarily as a result of lower than  anticipated revenues.
The company said it lowered its revenue plan for the  balance of 1987 and also expects to record a loss for the  entire 1987 year.
