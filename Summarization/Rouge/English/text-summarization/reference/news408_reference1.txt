Chrysler Corp said Electrospace Systems  Inc agreed to be acquired under a merger agreement in which  Chrysler will tender 27 dlrs a share for 100 pct of the  Richardson, Texas-based defense electronics contractor.
It said total cost to Chrysler to buy all of the  outstanding stock would be about 367 mln dlrs.
Chrysler said Electrospace will help its Gulfstream  operations grow in military and commercial aircraft sales.
Rather, they will operate as "sister companies,"  it said.
Chrysler said its tender offer is expected to begin by June  25 and will be managed by First Boston Corp.
