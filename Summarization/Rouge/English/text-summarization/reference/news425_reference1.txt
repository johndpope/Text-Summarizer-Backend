Republic Savings and Loan  Association and &lt;Pioneer Savings> of Racine, Wis., said they  have signed a definitive agreement to combine the two  associations into a publicly held holding company to be called  Republic Capital Group Inc.
The combination of Republic's 459 mln dlrs in assets with  Pioneer's 125 mln dlrs would make the new holding company the  fifth largest savings and loan organization in Wisconsin, they  said.
The associations said the move is subject to approval by  the Federal Home Loan Bank Board and the Wisconsin Commissioner  of Savings and Loan, as well as Republic's shareholders and  Pioneer's depositors.
