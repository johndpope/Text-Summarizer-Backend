Union Carbide Corp is looking to  acquisitions and joint ventures to aid its chemicals and  plastics growth, according the H.W. Lichtenberger, president of  Chemicals and Plastics.
Describing this as a major departure in the company's  approach to commercial development, he told the annual new  business forum of the Commercial Development Association "We  are looking to acquisitions and joint ventures when they look  like the fastest and most promising routes to the growth  markets we've identified."      Not very long ago Union Carbide had the attitude "that if  we couldn't do it ourselves, it wasn't worth doing.
