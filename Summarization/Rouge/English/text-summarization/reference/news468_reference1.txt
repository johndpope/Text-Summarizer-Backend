Soviet sugar imports in October and  November were significantly higher than in the same period of  the year before, according to figures received by the  International Sugar Organization.
For the first 11 months of 1986, Soviet imports totalled  5.12 mln tonnes, against 4.30 mln in the same period of 1985.
Of  the imports in the January/November period, those from Cuba  were up to 3.81 mln tonnes from 3.65 mln and from Thailand to  292,808 tonnes from 22,800.
The January/November  export total of 289,232 compares with 165,859 tonnes in the  first 11 months of 1985.
