Revlon Group Inc told the Securities  and Exchange Commission it had net profits of 16,465,000 on  sales of 1.61 billion dlrs for the year ended Dec. 31, 1986.
Earlier today, MacAndrews and Forbes Group Inc began an  18.50 dlr a share cash offer for all common stock of Revlon it  does not own.
For 1986, Revlon said the loss applicable to common stock  after preferred dividends is 9.5 mln dlrs.
Revlon said it had a 1986 operating profit of 64.3 mln  dlrs, compared to a loss of 13.3 mln dlrs in the year earlier  period.
The 1986 net includes a provision for estimated loss on  dispositions of 57.0 mln dlrs, compared to 15.1 mln dlrs the  year earlier.
