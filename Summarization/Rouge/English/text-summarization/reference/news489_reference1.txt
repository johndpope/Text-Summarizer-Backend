The Surinam Aluminum Company  (SURALCO) registered a 75 pct drop in alumina exports in  February after its operations were shut down by worker violence  and guerrilla sabotage, the official Surinam News Agency, SNA,  reported.
SNA said Suralco's alumina exports dropped to 23,869 tonnes  in February from 92,852 tonnes in January.
Suralco's alumina refinery at Paranam, 18 miles south of  the capital, was shut down February 2 after workers destroyed  plant and computer equipment in a protest over layoffs at the  company's nearby aluminum smelter.
