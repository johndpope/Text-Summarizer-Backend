Standard Oil Co &lt;SRD> said in a brief  announcement issued after a meeting of its board of directors  that British Petroleum Co PLC &lt;BP.L> (BP) has extended its 70  dlr per share tender offer until midnight May 4.
The offer for the 45 pct of Standard shares not owned by BP  had been due to expire midnight April 28.
Standard Oil said discussions with BP concerning the tender  were continuing but provided no further details.
