The Department of Transportation  said it will allow USAIR Group to acquire up to 51 pct of  Piedmont Aviation &lt;PIE> voting stocks pending final approval of  the proposed merger of the two airlines.
An agency spokesman said that if USAIR, which has proposed  to buy all of Piedmont shares, controls more than 51 pct of the  firm's outstanding stock, it will have one week to sell those  excess shares.
The spokesman said the firm had asked the transporation  agency permission to buy all of Piedmont's voting stock, the  but agency decided to give approval for 51 pct of shares.
