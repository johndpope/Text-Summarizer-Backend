British Telecommunications Plc &lt;BTY.L>  expects to announce satisfactory results for its 1986/87  financial year ending March, chairman Sir George Jefferson said  in a third quarter statement.
In the 1985/86  financial year, pre-tax profit rose to 1.81 billion stg from  1.48 billion in the previous period.
Earlier, the company reported third quarter pre-tax profit  for the period ended December 31 of 506 mln stg compared with  452 mln a year earlier.
British Telecom shares were last quoted at 246-1/2p, level  with late yesterday, in the wake of results which were in line  with market expectations, dealers said.
