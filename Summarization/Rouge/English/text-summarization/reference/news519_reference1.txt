Leucadia National Corp said two of  its subsidiaries have acquired a 7.2 pct stake in Minstar Inc,  a corporation controlled by corporate raider Irwin Jacobs and  used by him in his forays to acquire stock in companies.
Since July, Leucadia said its companies have bought 947,800  Minstar common shares for a total of 24.7 mln dlrs.
Leucadia said it bought the Minstar stake to obtain an  equity position in the company and has no intention of seeking  control of it.
