Toshiba Corp &lt;TSBA.T> said it regrets its  plan to enter the U.K. Business facsimile and telephone market  may be caught up in a diplomatic row over the position of Cable  and Wireless Plc's &lt;CAWL.L> in the Japanese market.
"As a matter of timing it is regrettable that this has been  linked with the question of market access in Japan," a Toshiba  spokesman told Reuters.
The Toshiba spokesman said the sale of such equipment was  not comparable to Cable and Wireless' efforts to take a stake  in the new Japanese telecommunications firm.
