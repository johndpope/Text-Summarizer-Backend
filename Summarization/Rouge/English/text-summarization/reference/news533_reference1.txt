Mario Gabelli, head of New York  investment firm Mario Gabelli and Co, said he sold some shares  of GenCorp Inc &lt;GY> as part of a "portfolio rebalancing"  process to meet the needs of his more than 600 clients.
Regarding the company's shareholders meeting Tuesday,  Gabelli told Reuters, "there was nothing said that caused me to  sell or buy." He still thinks "values are 140 dlrs per share or  more" and has clients that asked to buy more.
"We wanted to remove our clients from the process  unfolding between GenCorp and Wagner and Brown," he said.
