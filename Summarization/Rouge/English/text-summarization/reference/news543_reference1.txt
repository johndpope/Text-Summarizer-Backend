The new interest rate of 7/8 pct over  Libor on Venezuelan public sector debt payments goes into  effect today, Finance Minister Manuel Azpurua said .
The new rate is among changes agreed last month to the 20.3  billion dlr public sector debt rescheduling.
He told reporters the term sheet detailing the changes in  the rescheduling has already been endorsed by the 13-member  debt steering committee and is now being sent to the country's  460 creditor banks for their approval.
