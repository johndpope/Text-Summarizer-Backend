Liberty Financial Group Inc, the  parent of Liberty Savings Bank said it has signed a definitive  agreement to be acquired by Equimark Corp &lt;EQK>, the parent of  Equibank, for 48 dlrs a share of Liberty.
The transaction is structured as a merger of Liberty with a  duly-formed unit of Equimark, the company said.
Liberty shareholders will receive about 48 dlrs per share  uopn the merger, the company said.
