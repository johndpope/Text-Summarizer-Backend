The Singapore government has taken  over 82 mln dlrs worth of Goodwood Park Hotel Ltd shares and  loan stock certificates belonging to the company's chairman,  Khoo Teck Puat, banking sources said.
The Goodwood securities are now held by the Commercial  Affairs Investigation Department, the banking sources said.
The government move is aimed at protecting Goodwood's  minority shareholders and securing Goodwood deposits at the  NBB, the banking sources said.
