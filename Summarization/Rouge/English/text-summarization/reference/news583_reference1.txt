Petroleos de Venezuela, S.A. said it  will sign a contract March 17 to buy a half interest in a  Corpus Christi, Texas refinery and related operations.
The contract, to be signed by PDVSA and Champlin  Petroleum's parent company, the Union Pacific Corp &lt;UNP>, will  create a new joint venture called Champlin Refining.
The state oil company said PDVSA will pay on the order of  30 mln dlrs for the half interest in Champlin.
