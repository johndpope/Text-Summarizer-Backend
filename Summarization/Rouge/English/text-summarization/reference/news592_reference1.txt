Finance Minister Edouard Balladur,  Industry Minister Alain Madelin and Telecommunications Minister  Gerard Longuet met for more than an hour today to discuss the  imminent sale of the French telephone switching group &lt;Cie  Generale de Constructions Telephoniques>, a spokesman for  Longuet said.
