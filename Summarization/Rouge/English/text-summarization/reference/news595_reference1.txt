Under the plan its board declared a dividend distribution  of one right for each outstanding common share held as of March  16.
It said each right entitles holders to purchase a unit of  1/100 of a share of newly authorizes series A participating  preferred at 75 dlrs per unit.
Humana said the rights become effective after an entity  acquires 20 pct or more of its outstanding common or tenders  for 30 pct of its stock.
After such an acquisition, then each  right entitles holders to purchase securities of the company or  an acquiring entity having a market value of twice the right's  exercise price.
