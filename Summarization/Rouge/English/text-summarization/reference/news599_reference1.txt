British package courier &lt;Interlink  Express Plc> does not plan to bid for the whole or part of    Purolator Courier Corp &lt;PCC.N>, a spokesman said.
Purolator last year topped turnover of 465  mln dollars, the spokesman said.
He categorically denied press reports suggesting  Interlink was developing a buyout bid.
The reports said the proposed bid would be a price above  the 35 dlrs per share offered by &lt;E.F. Hutton LBO Inc>.
