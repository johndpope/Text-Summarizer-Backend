Castle and Cooke Inc said it would  take a fourth quarter after-tax loss of 33.9 mln dlrs from the  previously reported sale of its Flexi-Van container leasing  business to Itel Corp &lt;ITEL> for about 215 mln dlrs.
For the year, however, the diversified holding company  reported a net profit of 43.9 mln dlrs, or 56 cts a share,  versus 46.4 mln dlrs, or 56 cts a share, in 1985.
