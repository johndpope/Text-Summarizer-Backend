Schweizerische Aluminium AG, Alusuisse,  plans to reduce share and participation certificate capital by  50 pct to cover losses in 1986 and those carried forward from  the previous year, chief executive Hans Jucker said.
The losses in 1986 and those carried over from 1985 made it  necessary to reduce capital by 50 pct, he said.
Trading in Alusuisse shares was suspended on the Zurich  stock exchange after today's announcement by the company that  it would cut its share capital by 50 pct, the bourse said.
