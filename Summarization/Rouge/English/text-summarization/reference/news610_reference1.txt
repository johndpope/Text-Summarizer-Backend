Hillsdown Holdings Plc &lt;HLDN.L> said  early results for 1987 were encouraging, and the combination of  its investment and acquisition strategies enabled it to look  forward confidently for an excellent result for the year.
The placing of 82.5 mln shares last year raised 160.7 mln  stg and enabled shareholders' funds to more than double to 352  mln at year-end.
Net borrowings were 20 pct of shareholder's funds and the  group had listed investments of 47.1 mln.
The company said it had bought a total of 40 companies  during the year for a total price of some 180 mln stg.
