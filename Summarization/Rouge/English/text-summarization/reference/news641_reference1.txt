A U.S. Commerce Department spokesman  said the agency would have no comment on Schlumberger Ltd's  announcement that it had terminated an agreement in principle  to sell its Fairchild Semiconductor Corp unit to Fujitsu Ltd.
Commerce chief spokesman B.J. Cooper said the department  had had no contact with Schlumberger about the deal.
A Defense Department spokesman said the agency was unaware  of Schlumberger's announcement and would have no immediate  reaction.
