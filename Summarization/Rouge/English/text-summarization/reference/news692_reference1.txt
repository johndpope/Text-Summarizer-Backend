Alcan Australia Ltd &lt;AL.S> said it will  make a 39.3 mln N.Z. Dlr cash bid for all the issued shares of  &lt;Alcan New Zealand Ltd> at 1.80 N.Z. Dlrs each with a  four-for-three share alternative.
The remainder of  Alcan New Zealand's totalled issued 21.84 mln shares are  broadly held while Alcan Australia's are primarily held by  institutions.
Alcan Australia would invite New Zealand representation to  its board and would apply to list its shares on the New Zealand  Stock exchange, Davis said.
