OPEC member Ecuador adopted austerity  measures to conserve fuel after oil production was paralyzed by  a strong earthquake.
Energy and Mines Minister Javier Espinosa announced on  television the country would cut domestic fuel sales by 30 pct.
The government's austerity measures ban the sale of  aviation fuel to foreign airliners on international routes and  no fuel will be available for ships owned by foreign lines.
Ecuador also banned the sale of petrol on weekends and  holidays and limited sales on weekdays, an Energy and Mines  Ministry communique said.
