Home Federal said the meeting is to approve a definitive  agreement signed May 26, calling for each share of Home  Federal's common stock to be exchanged for about 40 dlrs in  market value of Barnett common stock.
At 40 dlrs a share, Home Federal said the total market  value of the transaction would be about 175 mln dlrs.
Home Federal said notice of the meeting and related proxy  materials, which has been filed previously with the Securities  and Exchange Commission and the Federal Home Loan Bank board,  were mailed on June 26, to holders of record on June 15.
