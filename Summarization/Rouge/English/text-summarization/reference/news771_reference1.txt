The report states that total net proved reserves at year's  end stood at 2.5 billion barrels, an increase of 94 mln barrels  or four pct above the previous year and detailed data show that  the gains resulted from an increase in net proved reserves of  natural gas liquids.
Mobil said gains were in Indonesia where a sixth production  facility began operation in October with a capacity to  manufacture 1.7 mln tonnes of liquified natural gas.
Detailed data on reserves shows that U.S. net proved  reserves of crude oil fell to 837 mln barrels from 853 mln  barrels in 1985, natural gas liquid reserves were also lower in  1986 from the previous year.
