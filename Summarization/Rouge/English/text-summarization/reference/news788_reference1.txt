&lt;Guardian-Morton Shulman Precious  Metals Inc> said Morton Shulman and Guardman Investment  Management Services Inc agreed in principle for Andrew Sarlos  to make a takeover bid for all special shares and series II  warrants of Guardian-Morton, subject to regulatory approval and  completion of definitive documentation.
Price to be offered for the Guardian-Morton special shares  under the takeover bid will be 90 pct of the net asset value of  the special shares at the time of the bid, and two dlrs for  each series II warrant, the company said.
