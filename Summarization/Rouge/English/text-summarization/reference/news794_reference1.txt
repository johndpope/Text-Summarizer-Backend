Diplomatic sources here said several recent actions by EC  countries bear witness to a new disillusionment with the  willingness, or at least the ability, of the Japanese  government to reduce its massive trade surplus with the EC.
However, they said an all-out trade war may be far off, as  EC states know they would suffer almost as much as Japan.
The EC tariffs, which would involve renouncing obligations  entered into with the world trade body GATT, would be designed  to stop a diversion of exports to the EC market from that of  the U.S.
