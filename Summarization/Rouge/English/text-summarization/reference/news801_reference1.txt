Danforth said he continues to see value in a merger between  Westinghouse's radio operation and those of NBC.
Danforth reaffirmed that Westinghouse is interested in  acquisitions, saying the company is leaning toward acquisitions  in the 300 mln to 500 mln dlr range.
Danforth, who earlier today said he expects earnings growth  in the double-digit range through 1989, said he is comfortable  with analysts' predictions of 4.80 dlrs to 5.30 dlrs a share  this year.
In 1986 the company earned 4.42 dlrs a share.
