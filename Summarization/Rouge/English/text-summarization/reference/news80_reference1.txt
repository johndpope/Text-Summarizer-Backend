Itel Corp said it obtained commitments  from a syndicate of banks for a six-year secured loan of about  325 mln dlrs and had separately filed registration statements  for two public offerings for a total of 150 mln dlrs to fund  its December 1986 acquisition of &lt;Anixter Bros Inc>.
It said a portion of the proceeds from the offerings,  together with the proceeds form the new bank loan, wll be used  to repay the 395 mln dlr bridge loan Merrill Lynch and Co Inc  &lt;MER> provided for Itel to buy Anixter.
