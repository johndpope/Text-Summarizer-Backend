Commission sources said any new system would cover only  heavy products representing about 45 pct of the market, instead  of 65 pct under the present system.
Ministers hope to approve a closure program when they meet  again in Brussels on June 1, the sources said.
Industry Commissioner Karl-Heinz Narjes told ministers  capacity was 30 mln tonnes in excess of requirements and that  this excess ought to be eliminated by the end of 1990.
