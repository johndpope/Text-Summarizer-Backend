Italy's gross domestic product (GDP) will  grow three pct in real terms this year and 2.7 pct in 1988,  said economic information company Data Resources Europe Inc  (DRI).
Italy's budget ministry said yesterday that GDP rose 2.7  pct in real terms in 1986.
DRI estimates that foreign demand for Italian products  should grow by a 3.2 pct this year following last year's 6.2  pct increase.
