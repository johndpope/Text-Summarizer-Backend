Loans and advances from the Dutch  central bank to the commercial banks were barely changed at  12.9 billion guilders in the week up to and including March 2,  the central bank weekly return showed.
The Treasury's account with the bank dropped 1.3 billion  guilders.
They expect the money market shortage to continue around 12  billion guilders this week.
The weekly return showed total Dutch gold and currency  reserves rose 11.3 mln guilders to 56.0 billion guilders.
