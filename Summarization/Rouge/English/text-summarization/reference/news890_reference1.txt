H.J. Heinz &lt;HNZ.N> chairman Tony O'Reilly  would be interested in buying Guinness PLC &lt;GUIN.L>'s brewery  division if it were for sale, a spokesman said.
The spokesman, reacting to Irish and British press reports,  said "He continues to be interested were the group to offer the  brewery side of Guinness for sale.
The deal would mean buying the Dublin, London, Nigerian and  Malaysian breweries because "It could only be sold as an  integral unit if it was going to be sold at all," O'Reilly said.
