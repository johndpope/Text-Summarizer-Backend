Venezuela will back Ecuador's bid to  raise its OPEC quota above 221,000 barrels per day (bpd)  because it is a justifiable demand, Venezuelan Energy Minister  Arturo Hernandez Grisanti said on Monday.
"We should recognize  Ecuador's right to increase its quota ...
Hernandez Grisanti said he understood Ecuador would  formally submit the request for a higher quota at the next  ministerial conference of the Organization of Petroleum  Exporting Countries (OPEC) opening December 9 in Vienna.
