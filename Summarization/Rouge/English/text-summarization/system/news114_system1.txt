Decision Industries Corp said it  expects to report an operating loss of 19-24 cts a share for  the first quarter ending March 31 mainly due to lower profit  margins and higher sales and marketing costs.
In the 1st quarter of last year, when the company's quarter  ended Feb 28, 1986, it earned 957,000 dlrs or 10 cts a share on  revenues of 45 mln dlrs.
Decision also said it entered into an agreement to sell its  International Computerized Telemarketing Inc subsidiary to an  investor group headed by the unit's senior management.
