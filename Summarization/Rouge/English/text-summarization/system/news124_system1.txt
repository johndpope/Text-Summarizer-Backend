The joint committee of Taiwan's maize  importers awarded contracts to five U.S. Companies for seven  shipments totalling 340,000 tonnes of maize for delivery  between September 1 and December 20, a committee official said.
United Grain Corp of Oregon won two contracts for the  supply of 110,000 tonnes, priced between 92.44 and 96.00 dlrs  per tonne, for September 1-15 and November 5-20 delivery.
Cargill Inc of Minnesota also took two shipments totalling  110,000 tonnes, priced between 93.45 and 94.65 dlrs per tonne,  for October 1-15 and December 5-20 delivery.
Elders Grain Inc of Kansas took a 33,000 tonne shipment, at  96.06 dlrs per tonne, for December 1-15 delivery.
