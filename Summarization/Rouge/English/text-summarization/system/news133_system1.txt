Moderate to heavy rain continued  over early double-crop rice areas in eastern Guangxi,  Guanghdong, eastern Hunan, Jiangxi, Fujian, and Zhejiang in the  week ended March 28, the Joint Agricultural Weather Facility of  the U.S. Agriculture and Commerce Departments said.
In the North China Plain, dry weather covered winter wheat  in the vegetative stage, and rain is needed to meet increasing  crop moisture requirements, it said.
