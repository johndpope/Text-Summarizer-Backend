An estimated 1.2 mln metro users in  Madrid were stranded today as striking railway workers halted  the underground transport system, industry sources said.
Some 10,000 pitmen in the northern province of Leon entered  the second day of an indefinite stoppage to demand wage rises  and a five-day working week, the sources said.
Paramilitary police were guarding steel mills at Reinosa,  in northern Spain, from the daily protests against planned job  cuts.
