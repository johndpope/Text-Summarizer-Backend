Total Petroleum NA &lt;TPN> shut down  several small crude oil pipelines operating near the  Texas/Oklahoma border last Friday as a precaution against  damage from local flooding, according to Gary Zollinger,  manager of operations.
One other small pipeline that Total also closed has a  capacity of 3,000 to 4,000 bpd and crosses the Red River in  Fargo, Texas, Zollinger said.
