Japan's consumer price index (base 1985)  was unchanged at 99.7 in February from a month earlier, the  government's Management and Coodination Agency said.
In January, the index fell 1.1 pct from a year earlier, the  first drop of over one pct since a 1.3 pct drop in September  1958.
The unadjusted consumer price index for the Tokyo area  (base 1985) in mid-March rose 0.4 pct from a month earlier to  100.6, reflecting higher vegetable prices.
The index fell 0.3 pct year on year, the third consecutive  yearly drop, reflecting lower food and utility costs.
