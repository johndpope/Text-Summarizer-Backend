New Zealand's broadly defined,  seasonally adjusted M-3 money supply grew an estimated 3.6 pct  in December after rising a revised 2.4 pct in November and 4.04  pct in December last year, the Reserve Bank said in a  statement.
Narrowly defined year-on-year M-1 growth was 15.89 pct  against a revised 27.52 pct in November and 12.3 pct a year  earlier.
Year-on-year private sector credit grew 30.68 pct in  December against a revised 22.30 pct in November and 23.2 pct  in December 1985.
Private sector credit grew to 22.24 billion  dlrs from a revised 20.92 billion in November and 17.01 billion  in December 1985.
