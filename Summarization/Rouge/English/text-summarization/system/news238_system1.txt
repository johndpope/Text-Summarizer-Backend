France's current account payments surplus  slipped to a provisional 300 mln francs, seasonally adjusted,  in January from a downward revised surplus of 3.8 billion in  December, the Finance Ministry said.
The December figure was revised from a provisional 4.8  billion franc surplus reported a month ago.
The current account showed an adjusted surplus of 6.1  billion francs in January last year, and an unadjusted deficit  of one billion.
