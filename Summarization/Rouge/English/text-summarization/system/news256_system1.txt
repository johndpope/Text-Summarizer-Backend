The National Weather Service said  warnings of gale force winds remained in effect over lakes  Erie, Huron and Michigan.
Winds gusting to 45 mph  caused four to six foot waves along the western and southern  shores of Lake Michigan during the morning.
Advisories for low wind chill temperatures have also been  posted across portions of upper Michigan and northern lower  Michigan.
Snow also extended across  northern Wisconsin, upper Michigan and northern lower Michigan.
