Zaire agreed to limit its tin  exports to 1,736 tonnes for 12 months from March 1 in line with  an Association of Tin Producing Countries (ATPC) plan to curb  exports, the ATPC said.
ATPC Executive Director Victor Siaahan told Reuters he  received a telex from Zaire indicating its willingess to take  part in the plan to limit total ATPC exports to 96,000 tonnes  for a year from March 1.
Siaahan said Zaire is expected to produce 1,900 tonnes of  tin in calendar 1987, and that in 1986 its output and exports  were about 1,200 tonnes.
