West German Finance minister Gerhard  Stoltenberg said the meeting on Monday with U.S. Treasury  Secretary James Baker underscored the determination of the U.S.  And West Germany to continue close cooperation to stabilise  foreign exchange rates.
Stoltenberg said that he, Baker and Bundesbank President  Karl Otto Poehl had a very constructive discussion and had all  reached a positive evaluation of the Louvre accord during their  meeting on Monday.
