Davy McKee Ltd, a subsidiary of U.K.  Engineering and contruction company Davy Corp Plc, has been  awarded a contract worth around 15 mln stg for the design of a  zinc and lead smelter in India, a spokesman for the company  said.
The contract is being funded by Britain's Overseas  Development Administration as part of a total grant of 73.65  mln stg to India's state-owned Hindustan Zinc Ltd.
The grant is for the development of a major zinc and lead  mine and associated smelting complex in Rajastan, North West  India, the agency said.
The agency said the project was expected to account for 44  pct of India's total production of zinc.
