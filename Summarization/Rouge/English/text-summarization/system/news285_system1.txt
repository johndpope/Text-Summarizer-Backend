&lt;Laurentian Group Corp> said it  expects 1987 earnings per share will show about the same  proportionate increase as in 1986.
Last year, the company had 25.7 mln dlrs operating profit,  or 76 cts per share, up from 11.1 mln dlrs, or 59 cts per  share, in 1985.
Laurentian Capital Corp, which recently acquired two small  U.S. life insurance companies, had 1986 profit of 1.1 mln U.S.  dlrs, or eight cts per share, compared to seven mln U.S. dlrs,  or 68 cts per share.
The company also may expand this year in continental  Europe, in conjunction with La Victoire, a French insurance  company, he said.
