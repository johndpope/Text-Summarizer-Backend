In a statement released by its London office, Minpeco said  Peruvian customs figures for bismuth exports for the first five  months were 574.8 tonnes compared with 160.7 tonnes in the same  period of 1986 and 483.4 tonnes during the whole of 1986,  Minpeco said.
Sales to the U.K., West Germany, France and other Western  and Eastern European countries are hidden under the heading  Holland as Minpeco sells to customers in these countries from  stocks the company normally holds in Rotterdam, the statement  said.
