Diversified building materials group  Monier Ltd &lt;MNRA.S> said talks are taking place which may lead  to Britain's Redland Plc &lt;RDLD.L> making an offer for the  Monier shares it does not already hold, chairman Bill Locke  said.
Redland already holds about 49 pct of Monier's 156.28 mln  issued shares, he said in a brief notice to the Australian  Stock Exchange.
Monier is the largest concrete roof tile manufacturer in  Australia, the U.S. And New Zealand and the world's largest  marketer of fly ash, according to its annual report for 1985/86  ended June 30.
