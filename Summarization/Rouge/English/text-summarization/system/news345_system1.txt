Cook United INc said it expects  to report a loss a loss of 16 mln dlrs, before an extraordinary  credit of 44 mln dlrs resulting from its Chapter 11  reorganization, on sales of 217 mln dlrs for the year ended  January 31, 1987.
For 1986, Cook reported a loss of 30.8 mln dlrs on sales of  257 mln dlrs for the prior year.
Cook also said it began preliminary talks with private  investors looking to buy a stock position in order to provide  Cook with additional cash and assist its posture in its  negotation with its banks.
