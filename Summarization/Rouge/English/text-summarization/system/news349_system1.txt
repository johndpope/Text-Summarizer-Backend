Japanese shipbuilders have applied to the  state's Fair Trade Commission to form a cartel to restrict  tonnage built to about half of total capacity for the year  starting April 1, officials of the Shipbuilders Association of  Japan said.
The Transport Ministry estimates new orders at 3.3 mln CGRT  in the year to March 1988 and 3.1 mln the following year.
The latest guidelines call for a ceiling  of four mln CGRT in the year to March 31.
