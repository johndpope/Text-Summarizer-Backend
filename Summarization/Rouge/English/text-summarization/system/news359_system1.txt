Joint venture talks that could  lead to Chrysler Corp &lt;C> taking a stake in Italian car maker  &lt;Automobili Lamborghini SpA> are continuing, a Lamborghini  spokesman said.
He told Reuters the two companies are discussing a number  of topics ranging from "a joint venture in the production area  to Chrysler becoming a shareholding partner" in the Italian  firm.
The spokesman declined to comment on whether Chrysler was  interested in acquiring control of Lamborghini or if the two  sides were close to an accord.
