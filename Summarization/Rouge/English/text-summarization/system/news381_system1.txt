Allied-Signal Inc said it  agreed to sell its Amphenol Products unit to a subsidiary of  LPL Investments Group &lt;LPLI>, a Wallingford, Conn., investment  company, for 430 mln dlrs cash.
LPL indicated that at closing, warrants will  be sold which, when exercised, will reduce its stake in the  subsidiary, Amphenol Acquisition Co, to 60 pct, the company  said.
LPL also said Merrill Lynch and Co has committed to buy 175  mln dlrs of subordinated and preferred debt in the new LPL  unit, according to Allied-Signal.
