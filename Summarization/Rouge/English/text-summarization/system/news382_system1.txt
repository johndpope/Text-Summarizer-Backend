American Dynamics Corp and  &lt;Meridian Reserve Inc> said they signed a definitive agreement  under which Meridian will buy 81.9 mln shares, or 51 pct, of  American Dynamics' common stock.
Under terms of the agreement, Santa Ana, Calif.-based  Meridian said it will pay Columbus-based American Dynamics one  mln dlrs in cash and notes over five years and about 500,000  shares of its common stock.
Meridian, an oil and gas company whose operations are  primarily in Oklahoma, said the acquisition will increase its  its consolidated assets to 30 mln dlrs and its contractually  committed gas reserves to more than 40 mln dlrs at discounted  present value.
