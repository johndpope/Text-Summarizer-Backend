J.C. PEnney Co Inc said it signed a  letter of intent to acquire one mln shares, or a 20 pct equity  interest, of Beeba's Creations Inc &lt;BEBA> for 18.75 dlrs a  share.
Penney said the agreement also calls for Beeba's, a major  supplier of junior sportswear, to work with Penny in the  establishment of junior specialty shops to be created in about  100 Penney stores in early 1988.
