Dlr deficit in February after a 2.54 billion dlr surplus  in January as imports climbed and exports slid, the Census and  Statistics Department said.
Imports rose to 24.12 billion dlrs, up 2.6 pct from  January's 23.52 billion dlrs and 42 pct above the 16.98 billion  dlrs recorded in February 1986.
Domestic exports slid 28.9 pct to 9.99  billion dlrs from January's 14.05 billion dlrs but were up 19.7  pct over the 8.35 billion dlrs recorded in February 1986.
