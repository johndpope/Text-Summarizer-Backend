Australia and New Zealand Banking  Group Ltd &lt;ANZA.S> said it will make a one-for-two bonus issue  from its asset revaluation reserve to shareholders registered  June 2.
The bank said that by lowering the dividend rate it expects  to maintain the value of dividend payout on the enlarged  capital at about the level of its last full year ended  September 30.
Shareholders will also be asked to approve changes in the  bank's articles of association to allow it to offer shares in  lieu of the interim dividend at a discount of five pct to the  market price.
