Iranian Oil Minister Gholamreza  Aqazadeh is in the United Arab Emirates (UAE) to discuss oil  prices and the general market situation, Iranian officials  accompanying him said.
Aqazadeh said Organisation of Petroleum Exporting Countries  (OPEC) members were agreed on holding production stable and he  reiterated the importance of maintaining oil prices.
The Iranian news agency, IRNA, quoted him as saying before  leaving Tripoli that OPEC should do everything possible to make  oil prices permanently stable.
