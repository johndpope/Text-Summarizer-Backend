Casino hotel operator and real estate  developer Donald Trump said he has agreed to purchase the Class  B common stock of Resorts International Inc held by the family  and estate of late Resorts chairman James Crosby for 135 dlrs  per share.
Trump said the shares he has agreed already to buy  represent a 78 pct interest in the Class B stock.
There are  also about 6,432,000 Class A shares outstanding, but Trump said  "I have no interest in the Class A shares."
In Atlantic City, Resorts said in a statement that the  Class B shares being sold to Trump represents 73 pct of the  combined voting power in Resorts.
