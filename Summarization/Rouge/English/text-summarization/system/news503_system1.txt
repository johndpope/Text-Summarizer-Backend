Most major U.S. banks today matched  Tuesday's quarter-point prime rate cuts by Citibank and Chase  Manhattan Bank, the first prime rate boosts since mid-1984.
Among the major banks which today announced prime rate  increases to 7-3/4 pct were Bankers Trust, Chemical Bank,  Continental Illinois National Bank, Harris Trust and Savings  Bank, Irving Trust, Manufacturers Hanover Trust Co, Marine  Midland, and Security Pacific National Bank.
Other banking companies announcing prime rate rises  included Pittsburgh National Bank, Texas Commerce Bank-Houston,  Provident National Bank, First National Bank of Boston, Florida  Federal Savings and Loan Association, and SunTrust Banks.
