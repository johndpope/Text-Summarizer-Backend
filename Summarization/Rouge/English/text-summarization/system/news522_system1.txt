Hartmarx Corp, following a year of  restructuring, continues to target record earnings for fiscal  1987, Chairman John Meinert told the annual meeting.
In fiscal 1986, ended November 30, 1986, Hartmarx  reported earnings of 24.8 mln dlrs, or 1.20 dlrs a share, down  from the prior year's 42.7 mln dlrs, or 2.25 dlrs a share.
He said the company's women's apparel continues to grow,  and Hartmarx has on the drawing board a Briar concept store  which will feature ties, shirts and some tailored clothing.
