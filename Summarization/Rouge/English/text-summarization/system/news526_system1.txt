Morgan Guaranty Trust Co of New York  plans an issue of 12,000 warrants for gold bullion at 425 dlrs  an ounce, lead manager Morgan Guaranty (Switzerland) AG said.
Each warrant, priced at 955 Swiss francs, entitled holders  to acquire five-ounce bars of .999 gold in the period from  April 30, 1987, to July 31, 1991, exercisable on Wednesdays.
A Morgan (Switzerland) official said that on the basis of  the maturity of four years and three months, the pricing  involved an implicit volatility of gold of 24.5 pct before the  warrants were in the money, compared with implicit volatility  of 30 pct for the Indosuez gold warrants and 26 pct for the  Citibank gold warrants recent issued with maturities of 18 and  24 months.
