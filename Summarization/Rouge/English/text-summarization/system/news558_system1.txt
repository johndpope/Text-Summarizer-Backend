Ing C Olivetti EC SpA &lt;OLIV.M> does not  exclude the possibility of investing in a semiconductor venture  currently under discussion between Italy's &lt;STET - Societa  Finanziaria Telefonica P.A.> and France's Thomson-CSF  &lt;TCSF.PA>, an Olivetti spokesman said.
He said that if Olivetti were approached by the two  partners involved and the financial conditions of any proposal  were considered interesting, the company did not exclude the  possibility of investing in the venture.
Stet and Thomson said last Thursday they were negotiating  an accord involving their respective subsidiaries &lt;SGS  Microelettronica SpA> and &lt;Thomson Semiconducteurs> in the  civil semiconductor field.
They said the accord, once concluded, would be put for  approval to the French and Italian authorities.
