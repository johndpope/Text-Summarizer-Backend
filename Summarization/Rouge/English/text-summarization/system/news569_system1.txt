New credit advanced by finance houses,  retailers, bank credit cards and other specialist providers of  credit slipped to 2.66 billion stg in January from 2.78 billion  in December - but remained close to the average level for  1986's fourth quarter, the Department of Trade and Industry  said.
Of the January total, 1.15 billion stg was advanced on bank  credit cards.
On a three-month basis, total advances in November to  January were 3.0 pct lower than in the previous three months.
January saw a rise of 300 mln stg in amounts outstanding to  finance houses, other specialist credit grantors and retailers.
