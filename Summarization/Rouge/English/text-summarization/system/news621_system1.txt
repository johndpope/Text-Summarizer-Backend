Bank of New England Corp reported  that pro forma first quarter earnings, which reflect its   combined operations under a pending merger with the Conifer  Group, rose to 60 mln dlrs, or 89 cts a share, from 49 mln  dlrs, or 75 cts a share in 1986.
Earlier, Bank of New England reported first quarter net  income, not taking the merger into account, rose to 1.04 dlrs  from 83 cts a share.
The 1987 pro forma first quarter results include  nonrecurring merger expenses of 4.7 mln dlrs.
