Unocal Corp said it raised the  contract price it will pay for most grades of crude oil 50 cts  a barrel, effective today.
The increase also represents the latest in a series of  increases that began with USX Corp's &lt;X> Marathon Petroleum  Corp's notification yesterday evening that, effective today, it  raised its crude postings 50 cts a barrel, bringing its  contract price for WTI to 17.50 dlrs a barrel.
Earlier today, Sun Co &lt;SUN>, Phillips Petroleum &lt;P> and  Diamond Shamrock &lt;DIA> also said they raised their crude  postings 50 cts a barrel, bringing their WTI contract price to  17.50 dlrs a barrel.
