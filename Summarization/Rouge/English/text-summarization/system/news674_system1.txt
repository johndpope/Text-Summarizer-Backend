Morgan Crucible Co Plc &lt;MGCR.L> said the  prospects for 1987 were encouraging, with orders and sales  significantly up on last year in all divisions.
It earlier announced a 6.1 mln stg rise in pre-tax profit  to 24.8 mln stg for the year to December 28.
Morgan said although profits in the electronics sector  improved to 1.0 mln stg from 100,000 stg previously, results  were nonetheless disappointing.
