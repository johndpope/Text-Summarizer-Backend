The company also said that new capacity brought onstream  last year replaced 120 pct of Mobil's production, which  declined by about four pct in 1986 from the previous year.
Detailed data on reserves shows that U.S. net proved  reserves of crude oil fell to 837 mln barrels from 853 mln  barrels in 1985, natural gas liquid reserves were also lower in  1986 from the previous year.
Net proved crude oil reserves also fell in Canada to 224  mln barrels and in Europe to 403 mln barrels from the previous  year's level of 231 mln barrels and 439 mln barrels,  respectively.
