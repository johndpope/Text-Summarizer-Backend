Extracts from a report by Italy's Budget  Ministry on the country's economic development in 1986 showed  gross domestic product (GDP) 2.7 pct higher in real terms than  in 1985 but a rise in unemployment.
On a current prices  basis, GDP was up 11 pct, totalling 894,362 billion lire in  1986 against 805,754 billion in 1985.
On the basis of data recently recalculated by the national  statistics institute Istat, Italy had a trade deficit in 1986  of 3,722 billion lire, the report said.
