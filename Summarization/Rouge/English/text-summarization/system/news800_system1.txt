Canadian Pacific Ltd said fourth  quarter 1986 operating profit rose to 91.6 mln dlrs, or 30 cts  a share, from 50.7 mln dlrs, or 20 cts a share, a year ago.
The company said a drop in 1986 full-year operating profit  to 150.1 mln dlrs from a restated 252.7 mln dlrs in 1985 was  due mainly to lower world oil prices.
The company said its CP Rail division reported 1986 net  income of 119.4 mln dlrs, compared with 133.4 mln dlrs in 1985.
