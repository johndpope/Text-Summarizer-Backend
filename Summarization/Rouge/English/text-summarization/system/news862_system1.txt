Argentine meat exports during  Jan/Dec 1986 totalled 220,911 tonnes, against 223,204 tonnes  shipped in the same 1985 period, the National Meat board said.
Shipments in tonnes with comparative figures for the 1985  period, in brackets, included: beef 120,184 (137,686), horse  meat 25,651 (33,892) and beef offal 50,100 (47,745).
Shipments in tonnes, with comparative figures for December  1985, in brackets, included: beef 14,384 (10,339), horse meat  1,609 (1,539) and beef offal 3,388 (3,098), the board said.
Main destinations for canned meat and cooked beef (bone in  equivalent) in tonnes, with figures for 1985 in brackets, were:  United States 88,300 (102,900), E.C.
