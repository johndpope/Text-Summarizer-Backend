Speaking by telephone from Sao Paulo, Peres said, "With its  decision, the U.S. government recognised the honesty of  Brazilian exporters, who never have the intention of practising  dumping."
In a final ruling yesterday, the U.S. government eliminated  the duties shipped by Sucocitrico Cutrale and cut to 1.96 pct  the duty on Citrosuco.
"We do not have in stock what we would like to have, but we  certainly have enough orange juice available to supply our  buyers," Peres said, declining to estimate the amount of the  current stock.
