&lt;Noranda Inc> said it began salvage  operations at its Murdochville, Quebec, mine, where a fire last  week killed one miner and caused 10 mln dlrs in damage.
Noranda said work crews have begun securing the ramp  leading into the zone where the fire was located.
Noranda estimated that the securing operation for the zone  will not be completed before the end of April.
Noranda said the Quebec Health and Safety Commission, the  Quebec Provincial Police and Noranda itself are each conducting  an investigation into the fire.
The smelter continues to operate with available  concentrate from stockpiled supplies, Noranda said.
