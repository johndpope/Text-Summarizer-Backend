CrossLand Savings FSB said it has  acquired Western Savings and Loand Co with the assistance of  the Federal Savings and Loan Insurance Corp.      CrossLand said Salt Lake City based Western has been  combined with its two Florida-based thrift subsidiaries.
The company said James J. Heagerty, chairman and chief  executive officer of CrossLand Savings FSLA in Bradenton, Fla.,  will serve in that capacity for the new subsidiary resulting  from the merger.
